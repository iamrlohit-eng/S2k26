About the Fest

Where JITD's spirit comes alive

Shrunga is the flagship annual cultural celebration of Jain Institute of Technology, Davangere. Every year, the campus transforms into a vibrant arena of music, dance, drama, art, and friendly competition.

Shrunga 2K26 promises bigger stages, more events, and the same heart that has made this fest a tradition to cherish.
